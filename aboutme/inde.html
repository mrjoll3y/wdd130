About Me

Callen Jolley

Greetings, I'm Callen Jolley, a proud resident of Idaho's enchanting small-town landscapes. I've had the pleasure of sharing my life with my amazing wife, Jocelyn, for the past 8 months. She's not only my partner in love but also in our shared passions for the gospel, the great outdoors, and animals.

Balancing the responsibilities of full-time work and my academic pursuits keeps life interesting. I'm currently immersed in my studies, majoring in Accounting and Applied Computer Technology at BYUI, with my sights set on graduating in 2025. This educational journey fuels my aspirations to one day establish my own businesses, particularly a restaurant and a wedding venture.

Beyond the world of numbers and technology, I find solace and excitement in the kitchen. Cooking is my creative outlet, where I explore flavors and whip up delightful dishes.

When I'm not wielding a spatula, you'll find me in the great outdoors, camping, hiking, and seeking thrilling adventures. Nature serves as a powerful backdrop for our gospel journey and personal reflection. Soccer, too, holds a special place in my heart, and I often enjoy a game on the field.

Looking ahead, my ultimate goal is to craft a self-sustaining homestead, where my entrepreneurial spirit intersects with my passion for sustainable living. As I embark on this transformative journey, I eagerly anticipate connecting with fellow adventurers and like-minded individuals throughout the semester!
