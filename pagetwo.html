Casual Adventure Package: - Enjoy a relaxed yet thrilling river experience suitable for all ages and skill levels. - Includes a guided rafting trip through scenic sections of Idaho's rivers, perfect for families and first-time rafters. - Professional instruction, safety gear, and a laid-back adventure ideal for a memorable day on the water.

Beginner Rapids Expedition: - Tailored for newcomers seeking an introductory rafting experience with gentle rapids and picturesque landscapes. - Expert guides lead the way, providing instruction and support throughout the journey. - Basic training, safety equipment, and a memorable float down the river, ensuring a safe and enjoyable initiation into the world of whitewater rafting.

Intermediate Thrill Seeker Package: - For those with some rafting experience looking for a more exhilarating adventure. - Navigate through moderately challenging rapids and explore the beauty of Idaho's rivers with experienced guides. - Advanced instruction, gear suitable for moderate rapids, and an adrenaline-pumping journey through captivating natural scenery.

Advanced Rapids Expedition: - Tailored for seasoned rafters seeking an adrenaline-charged adventure through Idaho's most challenging rapids. - Navigate intense rapids, steep drops, and exhilarating twists and turns, led by highly skilled and experienced guides. - Specialized equipment, in-depth training, and an unforgettable, high-thrills whitewater adventure for the daring and experienced rafter.

About Us

In the heart of our shared love for the great outdoors and a relentless thirst for adventure, my wife and I found our calling: launching a whitewater rafting company. With a bond forged by a mutual passion for exploration, nature's wonders, and the rush of adrenaline, we've set out on a journey to introduce others to the breathtaking experience of navigating Idaho's untamed rivers. Our story is one woven with the fabric of exploration, an unwavering connection to the wilderness, and a burning desire to share the thrill of whitewater rafting with fellow adventurers. Join us in celebrating the magic of the outdoors, embracing the pulse of adventure, and crafting unforgettable moments on the rapids of Idaho's stunning waterways.
